About Vanguard

WE are a esports team consisting of 16-19 students from King edward VI College who compete in the British esports student champs.

Our team competes in competitive titles such as: League of Legends, Valorant, Rocket League and overwatch 2. We train weekly and participate in regular scrims and tournaments to hone our skills and teamwork.
